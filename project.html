Our Project's

Project name : Otto Robot

Author: BG technology


Description:
Otto Robot is a user-friendly, open-source bipedal robot designed for educational purposes. It features a simple design, making it easy to assemble and customize with 3D-printed parts, servo motors, and an Arduino microcontroller. Otto is widely used in STEM education to teach robotics, programming, and electronics concepts. Its charm and accessibility have made it a favorite among DIY enthusiasts and maker communities worldwide.

Project name :Robotic Arm

Author: BG Technologies


Description:
Arduino-based robotic arms are mechanical manipulators controlled by Arduino microcontroller boards. These arms utilize servo or stepper motors to move joints and perform tasks. Arduino processes sensor inputs to determine arm positions, making them versatile platforms for learning robotics and implementing small-scale automation projects. While they have limitations in payload capacity and precision compared to industrial-grade arms, they offer cost-effective solutions for education, prototyping, and hobbyist endeavors.

Project name :Man following robot

Author: BG Technology


Description:
A man-following robot using Arduino is a mobile robot designed to autonomously track and follow a person. It integrates sensors and programming logic to detect and follow the person's movement. This technology finds applications in aiding navigation or serving as an educational tool to demonstrate sensor integration and autonomous navigation concepts.
